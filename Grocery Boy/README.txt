Grocery Boy
An on-demand delivery service for the Student Union shops at the University of Kent. 

Database is created/sorted using Firebase

Features
On campus residents will be able order items of less than 2kg (perishables not included
See reduced items in store
One click reorder: add all the items in your last order to your basket in one click
Pay via card, Android pay, or Roulette pay
Roulette: add friends to your order the name that gets called foots the bill
Track your order
Stretch goals
Get notified about out-of-stock items and choose a substitution on the fly
Rate your order
Speed
Order quality
